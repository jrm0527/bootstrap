ABOUT

OUR MISSION

Our Beer Garden aims to provide a magical experience in every interaction at every chance. We envision being a singular force in changing the long-held views of downtown Dover through positive development, strong community ties, and improving quality of life for our team, our neighbors, our customers. We value integrity, teamwork, excellence, and inclusion.

TEAM

  • Managing and Operating Owner Donny Legans is based in downtown Dover with his wife and two kids. A graduate of the US Air Force Academy, Donny will parlay his experience in the military as an aircraft maintenance officer and 14-year test and measurement national account manager in the telecom industry to lead this exciting new endeavor to new heights.
  • Consultant Josh Mann is a native Philadelphian and hospitality professional with over 20 years of experience. He’s held multiple General Manager positions throughout his career including Frankford Hall, the German inspired open-air restaurant he helped open in 2011 that has been voted top 50 in the country. Josh is helping with key areas of the project to include creation and implementation of company standards and systems, financial and HR systems and practices, hiring & training of all management teams, weekly operational meetings with each restaurant’s FOH & BOH teams, quality control and continued development of all managers and staff.


HISTORY

  • Purchased building/lot from Southern States Inc in Dec 2020
  • Applied for a change in land use and zoning from Planning Committee and City Council (7-1 votes on both measures)
  • Strong support from the neighbors with zero objections
  • Site Plan meeting passed 15 Nov 8-0 in favor; huge support from neighbors

VENUE

Flagship destination beer garden in Downtown Central Dover located at 92 N West street. Family friendly, kid friendly, dog friendly restaurant with vast outdoor space highlighted with communal tables, trees/landscaping, ambient lighting as well as recreational games (Bocce, Ping Pong, and cornhole) throughout the space. Wurst sausages, pretzels, fries, chopped salads and burgers are the main menu items. Hours of operation are 2pm – 10pm daily.

DIRECTIONS
